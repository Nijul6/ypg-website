Dala 2025

Dala 2025 Vision

Dala 2025 is a strategic vision developed to drive the goals of the PSN-YPG in advancing pharmaceutical excellence and youth leadership.

  • Promote innovation in pharmacy practice
  • Foster capacity building and professional growth
  • Engage with public health initiatives
  • Inspire the next generation of pharmacy leaders

The vision is built on sustainability, relevance, and empowerment, setting a roadmap that guides the PSN-YPG towards impactful programs across Nigeria.
